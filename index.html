About the event

Join the world’s most innovative thinkers in technology, October 4th, in the historic place of South India, Tanjore at Sastra University. Learn how the world’s most successful companies are using world-class technology to power their technical operations and eliminate downtime.

Are you a developer, a technology architect, an IT consultant, an IT student or a teacher from an IT university/faculty Or simply an IT enthusiast interested in the latest trends in open source and emerging digital technologies then South India Developer Fest is the best place that suits you.

It's a unique experience for developers, data engineers, technical geeks, and decision-makers to collaborate at the intersection of the latest technologies. Attendees will learn about the latest advances in React 360, HTML 5 games, Google Cloud, Digital Ocean, Spark AR, Alexa as well as real-world enterprise AI best practices.

Prepare yourself for South India Developer fest 2019 which promises to be the biggest and best show yet in Tanjore!
